How the App Works

Sign Up and Set Up Your Profile

Create your BlueWave Cleanup account to join a growing community of ocean defenders.

Join a Group and Get Notified of Local Cleanups

Once you've found a group in your area, join it to stay connected with local conservation efforts. The app will automatically notify you about upcoming cleanups organized by your group, so you'll never miss a chance to participate and make a difference

Track Impact in Real Time

After each cleanup event, the entire group will weigh their collected waste together. The total weight of the waste will be recorded in the app on the group's page.

Connect with Fellow Volunteers

After a cleanup, connect with other volunteers through the app, share photos, exchange stories, and stay motivated to keep making a difference.

Earn Rewards and Track Your Achievements

Every cleanup you participate in helps you climb the leaderboard and unlock badges. BlueWave Cleanup celebrates your commitment to conservation with achievements that showcase your dedication to protecting the ocean.

About BlueWave Cleanup

At BlueWave Cleanup, we are more than just an app we are a movement dedicated to protecting our oceans and coastlines through the power of community action. Our goal is simple: unite people to take collective action and drive real environmental change, one cleanup at a time.

Our Mission

We believe in the power of community to restore our coastlines. BlueWave Cleanup connects volunteers, local heroes, and environmental organizations, empowering them to drive meaningful change. By organizing cleanups and tracking waste, we can work toward cleaner oceans, stronger communities, and a healthier planet for future generations.

Our Vission

We envision a world where clean oceans and thriving ecosystems are the standard, not the exception. Through collaboration and education, we aim to inspire people to act, raising awareness and fostering sustainable practices that will protect our oceans for generations to come.

Our Values

  • Collaboration: The strength of our mission lies in the unity of individuals, communities, and organizations coming together to make a difference
  • Sustainability: We focus on creating lasting environmental impact, promoting practices that are sustainable for both the planet and its people.
  • Inspiration: We strive to motivate people to take ownership of their local environment, leading by example and making small actions count toward a global cause.
  • Empowerment: Through education, tools, and data, we empower communities to take charge of their surroundings and ensure their actions make a real impact.
